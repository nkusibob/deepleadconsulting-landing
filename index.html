Skill framework

A dynamic skill-based matching system for technical consulting and recruitment.

We evaluate needs and profiles through five skill clusters. This allows us to compute a relevant match between what a company needs, what a candidate offers, and what a team is missing.

What the system does

It maps selected skills to clusters, applies weights, computes a match score, and recommends the most relevant service focus for the request.

How the matching works 1. Selected skills are mapped to the five clusters. 2. Each cluster contributes to a weighted score. 3. The system estimates the strongest service fit based on the requested collaboration type and the chosen skills. 4. D.L.C receives a more structured request, making follow-up faster and more relevant.
